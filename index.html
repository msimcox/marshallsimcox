Dyacon, INC

Dyacon Inc produces the most practical, easy-to-use, and versatile weather stations on the market. All weather stations are made in the USA and come with real-time monitoring via DyaconLive. I received a paid intership as a web developer for Dyacon. I worked with a small team using GIT on the LAMP stack portion of DyaconLive.

Dyacon had a client request a simple, white-label blog without the hastle of domain registration or using more complex blog services. Our two man team at Dyacon created this blog and admin tool via LAMP and JS. Each blog for various customers is created as a sub-domain of dyacon.net.
